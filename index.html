Bergen a medieval city in Norway

Some places to learn about or visit in Bergen

Bryggen

Bryggen is probably Bergens most known landmark, it is also known as "tyskerbryggen" and "hansabryggen". It was founded in 1070 and was a central hub for the Hanseatic League.

Ulrikken

Ulrikken is one of Bergens seven mountains and out of the seven the tallest. You can travel to the top of the mountain via a cable car.

Trollhaugen

Trollhaugen is a famous museum dedicated to the classical composer Edvard Grieg (Born in 1843). Edvard Grieg was the first Norwegian classical composer to gain international acclaim.
